How To Play:

Setup:

  1. Pick the number of ships you would like to play with (1 to 6)
  2. Both Player1 and Player2 secretly place their ships

GamePlay:

  1. Starting with Player1, each player guesses a board tile where they think their opponent may have put one of their ships
  2. Opponents rotate firing at each other's ships until one of the two player's ships are all sunk. The person who still has ships left is declared the winner.
